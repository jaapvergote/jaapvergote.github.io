47. Delayed gratification

July 04, 2018

Delayed gratification is key to long term goal setting (VC’s, entrepreneurs, stock markets). Pleasure is the opposite, it’s instant gratification and gives us a dopamine hit (gambling, smoking, notifications, drinking).

If you approach short term pleasures with moderation, you can look ahead and plan for the long term gains. It requires self discipline, patience and a clear sense of why you embrace the delayed gratification.

By Jaap Vergote
