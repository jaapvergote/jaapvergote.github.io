50. Storytelling

July 10, 2018

Storytelling is one of the most important skills in today’s economy. In the early days we had an agrarian economy after which we went through an industrial economy and into a service economy. Today’s economy is an experience/connection economy. The future is a transformation economy.

  • A commodity business charges for undifferentiated products.
  • A goods business charges for distinctive, tangible things.
  • A service business charges for the activities you perform.
  • An experience business charges for the feeling customers get by engaging it.
  • A transformation business charges for the benefit customers (or “guests”) receive by spending time there.

If you can’t tell a captivating story you can’t connect with your customers and create authentic experiences and it will be hard to stand out.

By Jaap Vergote
