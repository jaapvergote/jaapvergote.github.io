31. On decision making

June 16, 2018

Making decisions is the single most important skill in life. The quality of your decisions determines everything else. So the question is: How to make decisions?

The basics:

  1. When you can’t decide, the answer is no. There are so many options. If you’re not sure, you should look for another option.
  2. When two options are 50/50, go with the option that’s going to be the hardest and most uncomfortable in the short term.
  3. In times of interpersonal conflict, choose the path that will leave you most internally calm. Peace of mind is the precursor for happiness.

Direction is more important than speed. Make better decisions.

By Jaap Vergote
