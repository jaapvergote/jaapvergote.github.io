42. Perspective

June 28, 2018

Perspective is always available. You can find it outside of your daily environment in a place/time of day you normally don’t go.

Once you spend the majority of your time in the place that initially gave you perspective, the power of it will fade. The key is to know what works for you and then go there frequently enough, but not too often.

By Jaap Vergote
