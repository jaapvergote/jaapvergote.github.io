39. Burning wood

June 25, 2018

Burning wood is a beautiful process. You revert the growth of a tree. All the energy the earth and sun put into growing the tree is being released in a short period of time.

It’s almost like storing sunlight. You could consider trees as the first batteries humans started using to apply energy when needed. This early battery tech is very basic; charging takes years, you deplete the battery in a couple of hours and you can only use it once.
