20. Money is a magnifier

June 04, 2018

Money only magnifies who you really are. You * $$$ = more you.

If you’re unhappy, it will make you more unhappy.

If you’re lonely, it will make you more lonely.

If you’re an asshole, it will make you a bigger asshole.

If you’re a giver, it will make you give more.

If you’re curious, it will open doors to explore further.

If you’re responsible, it will make you more responsible.

Who you magnify is vastly more important than how much you magnify. Be a good person to magnify, then the rest will follow.

By Jaap Vergote
