19. Clothes

June 02, 2018

Apple is the largest public company in the world measured by market cap.

Before there were iPhones the only thing we’d wear all day and every day were our clothes. Since the proliferation of smartphones it became part of your ‘outfit’. It’s always there as a default. Just like clothes.

The list of items in that category is quite small. Shoes, pants, shirt. And a smartphone.
