6X. Location

Your physical location vastly impacts the trajectory of your life.

Let’s consider every persons life as a thin line progressing through time. These lines cross when human interactions happen. On a world wide scale this web has high density knots in places where there are a lot of people. 

Large cities are places where you are in the middle of a high density knot, with a lot of interactions.
