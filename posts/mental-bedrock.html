Mental Bedrock

May 4, 2020

I've been thinking about mental bedrock lately. The foundation of your thinking. The axioms you build your life on. The things you know to be true.

For me, these are some of my mental bedrock principles:

1. Compound interest is the most powerful force in the universe

This applies to everything. Money, relationships, knowledge, skills. Small consistent improvements compound into massive results over time.

2. You are the average of the 5 people you spend the most time with

Your environment shapes you more than you think. Choose it wisely.

3. Most people are good

The vast majority of people want to do good and be good. Trust, but verify.

4. Everything is figure-out-able

Given enough time and effort, you can learn anything. The only real constraints are physics and time.

5. Life is not a zero sum game

Creating value for others creates value for you. Win-win is possible and preferable.

What's your mental bedrock?
