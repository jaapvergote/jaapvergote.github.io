30. Borders

June 16, 2018

Since we started mapping our planet we’ve had the tendency to divide the land (and oceans) we discovered with borders. Since WWII most of these borders have been stable.

Over time, these made up borders became real. Dividing cultures, languages and beliefs. What if we took away all borders, would we evolve towards a population with a wide, but largely mixed, variety of earthlings over time?

By Jaap Vergote
