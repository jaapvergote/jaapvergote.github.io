35. Writing

June 20, 2018

Writing is like downloading thoughts from your RAM memory to an external hard drive.

Your RAM memory (what’s in your consciousness at any given time) has only a certain amount of space. Downloading some ideas helps to structure them (process them with your CPU) and leave them there. You can always come back to them.

The benefit is that you now have free room for more ideas and thoughts.

By Jaap Vergote
