51. What you think about while running

July 13, 2018

Sports train the body and the mind. You know that feeling when you finished a run and it feels like you thought about everything and nothing and you just can’t really remember anything? That’s you being present, in the moment, not looking to the future or the past.

It’s also why high performing individuals need their workout, it allows them to clear the cluttered mind and be present.
