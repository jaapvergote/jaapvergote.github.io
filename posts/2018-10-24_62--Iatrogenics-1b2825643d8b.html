62. Iatrogenics

October 24, 2018

No action is sometimes better than action. Unfortunately we are biased towards action. There are no heroes in history lauded for not acting. All heroes did something which made them a hero.

In many situations, not doing anything will produce a better outcome than taking action. This effect is stronger when we try to intervene with complex systems we do not understand: our health/bodies, the economy, the environment,…
