46. Mountains

July 04, 2018

People like climbing mountains because it is simple. There’s the summit and there’s you standing at the foot of the mountain. You want to go to the summit. That’s it. You have a very simple and well defined goal and you can track your progress quite accurately.

The journey is physically demanding, mentally tough and possibly dangerous. The goal is simple: get to the summit.

By Jaap Vergote
