25. When you’re young

June 10, 2018

For all the twenty somethings out there, life can seem daunting. We often ask older, more experienced people: I am 2X, what should I do?

To me, it boils down to spending more time on making the big decisions. We spend a ton of time in a job, in relationships and living somewhere, but we don’t spend enough time making decisions about these parts of our life.

So when you’re 2X, this is what you should spend your time on:

  1. Decide where you want to live. The city you live in vastly impacts the trajectory of your life.
  2. Decide on who you spend time with. Relationships are very powerful and you can choose which ones you foster and which ones you don’t. Avoid negativism.
  3. Decide what job you do. You’ll learn a skill in a certain environment, but you have to think about what you want to learn and why.
