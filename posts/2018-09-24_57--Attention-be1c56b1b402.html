57. Attention

September 24, 2018

We all have a limited amount of time in life. We also all have a finite amount of mental energy. Our brain can only process a certain amount of information at any given moment.

Therefore it is not just time, but time X attention that matters. (~ think about the success of companies that leveraged technology to grab our attention and the value of our attention to them) Guard your attention and spend it wisely.
