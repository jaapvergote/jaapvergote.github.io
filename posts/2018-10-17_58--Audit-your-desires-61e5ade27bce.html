58. Audit your desires

October 17, 2018

Are your desires yours or are they copies of other people’s desires?

It’s important to audit what you want. Figuring out if it is something you really want vs something you want because people around you want it.

If you let other people project their desires on your life, you might never feel satisfied chasing and achieving those wants.

Even better: don’t desire too much. It’s not getting what you want, it’s wanting what you’ve got.

By Jaap Vergote
