36. Freedom

June 22, 2018

Freedom to is different than freedom from.

Freedom to is freedom to do anything you want. Freedom to do whatever you feel like, whenever you feel like.

Freedom from is internal freedom. It’s freedom from. It’s freedom from reaction. It’s freedom from feeling angry. It’s freedom from being sad. It’s freedom from being forced to do things. Striving for freedom from internally and externally

Freedom To is the freedom to say “Yes” and Freedom From is the freedom to say “No”. Both are very powerful.

By Jaap Vergote
