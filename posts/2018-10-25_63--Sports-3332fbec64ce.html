63. Sports

October 25, 2018

Why do so many people like watching sports? It’s because you can tie yourself to an entity and live its success, without the sacrifices.

It feels good when your team wins. For a moment you’re part of their aura of success. When they loose, you just don’t care that much or blame them. You try to seize the easy upside without putting in the hard work.

You get to feel ‘success’ for free.

By Jaap Vergote
