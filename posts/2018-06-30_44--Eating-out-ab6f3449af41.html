44. Eating out

June 30, 2018

When you go eat out at a restaurant the focus is the food. You’re there to eat and the restaurant is there to serve you food.

The breakdown of the restaurant’s cost structure tells another story. Most of what you pay goes towards rent and labor. The food itself is not that expensive, it’s the things that accommodate the service that are.

Next time you eat out, look around and understand what you’re paying for.

By Jaap Vergote
