45. Your circle of competence

July 01, 2018

Stick to what you’re good at. Delegate the rest. All of it.

Self awareness is key to know your strengths. If you haven’t found them yet, go explore, try multiple things, and don’t settle until you find it. Once you discover what you’re good at, look for areas where you can apply those skills. Then double down and go into tunnel vision. The results will compound over time.

Outsource your weaknesses, harness your strengths.

By Jaap Vergote
